about us

Who we are?

Located just over an hour away from Sydney, Lilac Valley Farm Stay is a simple, rustic farm stay accommodation in the hills of the Blue Mountains.

What you'll find here -

  • A beautifully restored, open plan luxury barn house
  • Extensive grounds situated on four acres of land
  • Farm animals including sheep, chickens, ducks and one very friendly goat named Rosie
  • An outdoor plunge pool just off the back verandah
  • A stunning cottage garden

The Lilac Valley experience

Relax and unwind with the family while you take in the fresh air at Lilac Valley Farm Stay.

Situated in close proximity to breathtaking natural landscapes, award winning cafes and restaurants, as well as world renowned hikes and scenic lookouts.

Whether you're looking to hike, take in the view, spend time with the animals or get cozy with your favourite book, there is something for the whole family at Lilac Valley Farm Stay.
