Who we are?

Lilac Valley Farm Stay is a simple, rustic farm stay accommodation in the hills of the Blue Mountains.

After years of hard work, the property now features a beautifully restored, open plan, luxury barn house, on four acres of land home to sheep, chickens, ducks and one very friendly goat named Rosie.

There is an outdoor plunge pool just off the back verandah, and a stunning cottage garden.
